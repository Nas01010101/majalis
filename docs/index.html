The idea

Debate is a spend decision — so learn when to spend

Agents in Majalis don’t argue about every answer. Evidence flows onto a shared, versioned belief board; questions are answered instantly from the board (zero-latency serving). The world model — trained on the society’s own logged episodes, not hand-set rules — watches the board and routes the expensive machinery (skeptic challenge → adjudication) only to beliefs that are probably corrupted.

Everything committed without a debate carries a conformal guarantee: E[error | accepted] ≤ α = 5%, holding empirically at 2.1% on 1,600 held-out decisions.

BRIEF · DECOMPOSITION & ROLES
Who does what, and why

Four typed roles across three Qwen backbones — extractor, proposer, skeptic, judge — author ≠ validator by construction. The skeptic decomposes every challenge into 2–4 binary sub-questions; a flash planner decomposes composite resolutions into sub-checks the orchestrator dispatches and aggregates.

BRIEF · CONFLICT RESOLUTION
Disagreement, adjudicated

Rumors that displace filings are flagged by supersession; the gate mandates adjudication, the judge rules from the provenance docket, and the fix is written back. Measured: the debate-ablated system’s 3 errors are exactly the poisoned beliefs — gated debate corrects all 3.

BRIEF · EFFICIENCY GAIN
Measured, not asserted

Identical event streams, one shared token+USD ledger per arm: 448/448 at a flat ~$0.005/q — 2.5× cheaper than the single agent at 32 steps and more accurate; vanilla 3×3 debate costs 12.6×.

The world model

Four organs, trained on the society’s own history

“World model” is a claim we test, not a metaphor: state estimation, forward dynamics, action-conditioned prediction, and planning in imagination — each measured, including where it fails.

01 · STATE ESTIMATION
What’s wrong now?

wrong_now: P(belief is incorrect) — 0.999 AUROC in-distribution, 0.937 on real LLM-built boards it never saw. Replaced a hand-set gate at 0.79.

02 · FORWARD DYNAMICS
What breaks next?

Multi-horizon hazard: P(overturned within k=1/2/4 batches) — AUROC 0.63/0.66/0.70, ECE < 0.01, 0% monotonicity violations.

03 · ACTIONS
What would debate do?

592 counterfactual (skip, debate) pairs mined from shared boards: debate helps 4.6%, hurts 0/592. Calibrated with Platt scaling (ECE 0.219 → 0.003).

04 · IMAGINATION
Plan before you pay

Maintenance policies auditioned entirely inside the model — 16,000 rollout decisions at $0 in API calls — before anything runs live.

Evidence

Planning in imagination — and two honest nulls

Zero-latency serving: questions answered instantly from the board; repairs happen only in maintenance windows with budget B. Which repair policy should get the budget? We audition all of them in imagination:

Maintenance-policy frontier

accuracy over 1,600 questions (100 seeds) · whiskers are Wilson 95% CIs · y-axis starts at 90%

Learned risk-ranked repair closes ~96% of the none→oracle gap at B=1. And the honest null: the hazard-discounted “planned” ranking never beats plain myopic risk repair in any tested regime — forward dynamics are learnable and calibrated, but at these horizons the decision value concentrates in calibrated state estimation. We report that, because it’s true.

Learned gate vs the hand-set gate it replaced

The learned gate fires half as often, catches more of the genuinely corrupted boards, and cuts false fires 17× — and both configurations still hold the conformal bound (accepted-error 2.1% vs 3.8%, each ≤ α = 5%). Same features, same threshold machinery; the only change is learning P(wrong) from the society’s own episodes instead of hand-setting it.

The economy argument

cost per 100 questions, all three arms at 100% accuracy on the pooled evaluation

Always-debate re-buys self-consistency at 14× the price. The WM-gated society fires debate on 7.8% of questions; maintenance mode moves every repair off the ask path entirely (0 ask-time debates, 112/112 correct, live on the box below).

What we don’t claim

Null #1: a planned two-branch argmax gate never beats the reactive threshold gate — and is ~2× less frugal (14.1% vs 7.8% fire rate). Null #2: hazard-discounted maintenance never beats myopic risk repair. Both replicated, both in the paper. The world model’s proven value is calibrated risk + economy, not (yet) long-horizon planning — that’s the honest frontier.
